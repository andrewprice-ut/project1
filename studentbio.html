Bio

Data analytics professional with a proven track-record of transforming complex data into insights that drive tangible results. A self-starter with a solid history of increasing revenue through carefully planned projects that optimize areas of greatest opportunity. My career began focusing on providing marketing analytics for a variety of clients such as Monster.com, Citi, and HomeAway where I identified multiple areas of opportunity that increased ROI and company revenue. Possessing a love for data has allowed me to build and manage my own analytics team from scratch at ad network Q1Media. Successfully, I transitioned into an analytics consultant role at Pivotal Analytics where I helped a number of small companies develop, manage, and analyze their company data. This experience encouraged me to develop and hone skills that are critical in data science, which include Python, SQL, VBA, Tableau, and much more. I believe that a well-rounded data analytics professional can add value to any industry if given the opportunity.
